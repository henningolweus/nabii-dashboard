## üìù Data Processing Notes

### Ticket Size Parsing
The script automatically:
- Extracts numeric values from "USD X million" format
- Converts all amounts to millions
- Marks "Undisclosed" and "Not disclosed" as NULL
- Uses `Deal1_Ticket` field for all ticket size data

### Sector Mapping
Added automatic mapping for 4 unmapped sectors:
- Biopharmaceuticals ‚Üí Healthcare & Education
- Pharmaceuticals ‚Üí Healthcare & Education
- Telecommunications ‚Üí Technology & Digital Services
- Tourism ‚Üí Manufacturing & Other

### SDG Extraction
Automatically parses SDG references:
- "SDG 7 (Clean Energy), SDG 13 (Climate)" ‚Üí [7, 13]
- Handles multiple SDGs per deal
- Aggregates across all deals for SDG wheel
